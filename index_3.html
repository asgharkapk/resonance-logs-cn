Why Players Use Resonance Logs

Everything Needed for High-End PvE

From progression raiding to casual dungeon runs, every system works together to provide useful, lightweight, and highly customizable combat information.

Real-Time DPS Analysis

Accurate live combat statistics with DPS, True DPS, damage share, skill breakdown, healing, tanking, support metrics, history, and encounter replay.

Raid DBM

Boss ability timers, mechanic warnings, countdown bars, encounter alerts, and visual indicators for supported raids and dungeons.

Interactive Minimap

Dungeon mechanic overlays with player positioning, safe zones, markers, puzzle solutions, boss locations, and real-time mechanic tracking.

Buff & Skill Tracking

Track cooldowns, buffs, debuffs, resources, factor progress, custom counters, teammate buffs, monster buffs, and character attributes.

Voice Callouts

Configurable voice announcements for buffs, mechanics, cooldowns, boss abilities, and important combat events.

Highly Customizable

Multiple themes, layouts, colors, profiles, overlay modules, fonts, compact modes, and complete UI customization.
